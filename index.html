Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Computer Science, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT, Beijing China), advised by Prof. Huadong Ma. I am also an affiliated member of Beijing Key Lab of Intelligent Telecommunication Software and Multimedia. I am currently majoring in Computer Science and Technology and expect to receive my PhD in November 2023.

My research covers the broad field of Internet of Things (IoT) and Mobile Computing, with special interests in 5G/Beyond-5G/6G networks, Time-sensitive Networking (TSN), and Network Measurement. My works have been published in top international conferences and journals such as ACM SIGCOMM, ACM MobiCom, ACM UbiComp, IEEE/ACM TON, IEEE TMC, and IEEE TWC.
