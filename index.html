Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Computer Science, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT, China), advised by Prof. Huadong Ma. I am also an affiliated member of Beijing Key Lab of Intelligent Telecommunication Software and Multimedia. Additionally, I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology with an expected completion date in November 2023. Furthermore, I received the B.S. degree from Qingdao University (QDU) in 2018.

My research covers the broad field of Internet of Things (IoT) and Mobile Computing, with special interests in 5G/Beyond-5G/6G networks, Time-sensitive Networking (TSN), and Network Measurement. My works have been published in top international conferences and journals such as ACM SIGCOMM, ACM MobiCom, ACM UbiComp, IEEE/ACM TON, IEEE TMC, and IEEE TWC.
